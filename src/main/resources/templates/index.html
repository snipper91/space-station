The International Space Station is a multinational research lab orbiting 220 miles above the surface of the Earth. By maintaining this orbiting lab scientists are able to research the effects of living in space on the human body, perform experiments that can only be done in zero gravity, and learn how to maintain a spacecraft for long periods of time. Astronauts have been living in the International Space Station since November 2000.
